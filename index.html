I am a postdoctoral researcher in set theory. This field is concerned with studying the properties of different infinities. At this moment, I am mostly interested in compactness principles, large cardinals and singular cardinal combinatorics. After receiving my PhD from the University of Freiburg under my advisor Heike Mildenberger, I am now a Visiting Assistant Professor at the University of North Texas, being mentored by John Krueger. For more information, you can look at my CV or my PhD Thesis.
